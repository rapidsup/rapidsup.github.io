ABOUT EVENT

What's up fellas!

Tired from quarantine? No races in foreseeable future? Join us at GITT (Global International Time Trial). We offer a simple way to race internationally with respect to self isolation rules.

Proceed with a simple registration procedure, install STRAVA app on your smartphone and start to compete with hundreds of top international athletes without leaving your home place.

You are welcome to start with the first event in GITT series - Kyiv Open ITT (SUP & K-1) organized by UFSS.

Participation in Kyiv Open ITT is absolutely free for all those who take part in the event, but we would be greatful for any possible donation which will be used for improvement of our service

Join up and go!

RULES

Common questions

  • IF YOU -16 OLD

    A data subject wants to register to an event "Kyiv Open ITT". After having declared that he is not 16 years old, he needs to provide a) his own e-mail address, which newsletters will eventually be sent, and b) his parent's e-mail address. After having subscribed, both the data subject and the parents receive an automated email, asking to confirm the subscription and confirm that the parents agree to such processing of their child personal data.

    Kyiv Open ITT Competition Regulations in K-1 and SUP categories: 1. Kyiv Open Individual Time Trial is an individual competition for everyone around the world 2. Registration with gitt.pro will start on 30.04.2020. 3. The entry fee is paid on a charitable basis. Each participant can pay any amount that he see as fitted. All raised money will be spent on the organization of this and the following UFSS sports events 4. The length of the distance - 5km, for all participants 5. The race route must form a closed loop (start and finish is at one place). The maximum allowed distance between the start and finish points is 50m 6. Each participant should stick with the safety rules on the water, and existing quarantine restrictions. 7. ITT (Individual Time Trial) competitions start on 16.05.2020 and end at 17.05 at 23.30 London time (UTC + 1) 8. All participants may complete the scoring attempt at any time convenient for them during the competition time specified in clause rrt 9. Participants must send the result of the scoring attempt to the Kyiv Open ITT Organizer Messenger no later than 24:00 on May 17 (UTC + 1). Screenshot of the tracking from the STRAVA application 10. The results are only recorded in the Strava application. In order to compete, you need to install Strava on your smartphone and register your own account. Most popular trackers (Garmin, Polar, etc.) can be synchronized with the Strava 11. Before attempting a test, select the type of activity (K-1 or SUP) in your Strava application that fits your class according to registration 12. Classes of participants: - SUP UNLIMITED (Men / Women) - SUP Inflatable (Men / Women) - K-1 Unlimited (Men / Women) 13. Age categories: Up to 15 years From 15 to 40 years 40+ 14.
