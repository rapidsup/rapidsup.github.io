ABOUT EVENT

Hello friends!

Tired from quarantine? No races in foreseeable future? Join us at GITT (Global International Time Trial). We offer a simple way to race internationally with respect to self isolation rules.

Proceed with a simple registration procedure, install STRAVA app on your smartphone and start to compete with hundreds of top international athletes without leaving your home place.

You are welcome to start with the first event in GITT series - Kyiv Open International Time Trial SUP & K1. This event is supported by Ukrainian Federation of SUP and Surfing (UFSS).

Participation in Kyiv Open ITT is absolutely free for all those who take part in the event, but we would be greatful for any possible donation which will be used for improvement of our service.

  • RULES & REGULATIONS

    Kyiv Open ITT Competition Regulations in K1 and SUP categories:

    1. Kyiv Open Individual Time Trial is an individual competition for everyone around the world

    2. Registration with gitt.pro will start on 30.04.2020.

    3. The entry fee is paid on a charitable basis. Each participant can pay any amount that he see as fitted. All raised money will be spent on the organization of this and the following UFSS sports events

    4. The length of the distance - 5km, for all participants

    5. The race route must form a closed loop (start and finish is at one place). The maximum allowed distance between the start and finish points is 50m. Participants must use the same route before and after U-turn to avoid wind and flow influence on final result

    6. Each participant should stick with the safety rules on the water, and existing quarantine restrictions.

    7. ITT (Individual Time Trial) competitions start on 16.05.2020 and end at 17.05 at 23.30 London time (UTC + 1)

    8. All participants may complete the scoring attempt at any time convenient for them during the competition time specified in clause rrt

    9. Participants must share the result of their scoring attempt to the Kyiv Open ITT Organizer email (gittpro2020@gmail.com) via STRAVA application no later than 24:00 on May 17 (UTC + 1).

    10. The results are only recorded in the Strava application. In order to compete, you need to install Strava on your smartphone and register your own account. Most popular trackers (Garmin, Polar, Apple, etc.) can be synchronized with the Strava

    11. Before attempting a test, select the type of activity (K1 or SUP) in your Strava application that fits your class according to registration. We are strongly recommend to watch our instructional video

    12. Classes of participants:

    - SUP UNLIMITED (Men / Women)

    - SUP Inflatable (Men / Women)

    - K1 Unlimited (Men / Women)

    13. Age categories:

    Up to 15 years

    From 15 to 40 years

    40+

    14. It is forbidden to use drafting or any mechanical propulsion except appropriate paddle (single-bladed for SUP and two-bladed for K1). The scoring attempt is made in accordance with ISA rules for SUP and the ICF Marathon races for K1. We look forward to the honesty of the participants

    16. Awards: Each registered participant who has passed the distance according to the competition rules will receive an individual electronic certificate with his own result.
